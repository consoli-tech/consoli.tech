Consoli Tech

Consoli Tech, LLC is a wholly owned subsidiary of Consolidated Utilities, Inc. We have a small team of developers who are currently working on the Aqua Certify Compliance Central Platform.

This platform streamlines compliance reporting for water and wastewater utilities in the state of Georgia. It is a cloud-based solution that allows for data collection with mobile devices. SCADA integration is forthcoming.

Aqua Certify is currrently in the beta testing phase being used by a small number of utilities in the state of Georgia. At a later point in time, we may open up the platform to more utilities. We are currently focused on meeting the needs of the utilities that are currently using the platform.
